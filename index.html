Explore the canvas as it moves the music and refresh the page to load in another song.

The blocks move according the various frequencies that the music is playing, creating vibrant colors and patterns

(1) Hold left-click on the mouse to rotate your view

(2) Hold right-click on the mouse to pan your view

(3) Scroll the mouse wheel to zoom in and out

(Please Use Firefox to run the page as it runs into issues when running on other web browsers)
